It all started with a dream...

The concept behind Blue Hour Photography started with a simple dream of mine. I wanted to be able to take those special moments in people's lives and turn those memories into physical objects that could be shared. No matter how important they are to us, memories fade. The thought of having such precious moments lost to time has always bothered me; I wanted to be the catalyst in stopping that.

Through my photographs, I aim to not only capture the look of an image. I want to capture a moment in time. I want my clients to be able to look at an image of their wedding and taste the cake on their tongue. I want them to look at a photo of their newborn baby and hear their first laugh. I want to capture the very essence of the moment with every click of my camera.

I started my business back in 2014 with nothing but myself, a secondhand DSLR camera, and hope for the future. I took on some friends and family as clients to build my portfolio, and I quickly started getting my name out into my local community. After having to take a hiatus due to Covid-19 in 2020, I'm finally starting to build back my clientele and work towards growing my dream even more!

My specializations

I specialize in mostly event photography, leaning towards weddings, proms, new baby sessions, and other events where the emotion of the moment is palpable in the air.

I am, however, also able to do headshot shoots for those professionals that really want their unique personality to shine through their photos.
